Workflow

How it Works

Three steps from resume to autopilot — no manual spreadsheet required.

Step 01

Sync

Upload your resume and profile. Career-Ops ingests your experience, proof points, and target roles into a single source of truth.

Step 02

Deep Analysis

Every offer gets A–G scoring: CV match, comp research, interview prep, and legitimacy checks — powered by your real metrics, not generic fluff.

Step 03

Autopilot Application

High-fit roles trigger tailored CVs, cover letters, and form drafts. You review — the agent handles the repetitive work.

System Features

Built for real automation,
not chatbot theater.

Career-Ops runs a background Playwright browser to verify live postings, fill application forms, and generate ATS-optimized PDFs — with zero-token portal scanning when APIs are available.

  • Playwright Browser Automation

    Headless Chromium verifies job liveness, snapshots JDs, and assists live application flows — you always approve before submit.

  • Automated Tracking Pipeline

    Every evaluation lands in your applications tracker with scores, reports, PDF status, and canonical follow-up cadence.

  • Quality-First Ethics

    Strong matches only. Low-fit applications are flagged — your time and recruiters' time stay protected.
